**UPDATED CONTRACT – WALLCRAFT DRYWALL, INC.**

**Project:** 6501 S. Sepulveda

**Effective Date:** September 10, 2024

**1. Project Overview:**

Wallcraft Drywall, Inc. will provide drywall installation services for the 6501 S. Sepulveda project, encompassing various unit types (Type I, Type III) and common areas.  All work will be performed in accordance with approved plans and specifications.

**2. Scope of Work:**

*   **Drywall Installation:** Installation of drywall in all unit areas, corridors, and common areas as detailed in the architectural plans.
*   **Metal Framing:** Installation of metal stud framing systems (1-5/8” & 2-1/2” gauges) as specified in the plans, including furred ceilings.
*   **Soundproofing:** Application of Dens Armor Glass Mat board in designated areas (corridors, joist bays, post & beams, shafts) as per the plans.
*   **Finishes:** Application of “Light Orange Peel” texture to amenity walls and ceilings, and a Level 5 smooth finish to unit walls and ceilings.
*   **Access Panels:** Labor for spreading and installation of access panels supplied by the client. ($40/panel)
*   **Waste Removal:**  Included in the base bid is the removal and disposal of all drywall and metal waste.

**3. Materials & Specifications:**

*   **Metal Studs:** 25ga & 20ga gauges as specified in the plans.
*   **Gypsum Board:** Standard grade.
*   **Soundboard:** P ABCO Sound Curb or equivalent.
*   **Texture:** “Light Orange Peel”
*   **Primer:** Prep Coat
